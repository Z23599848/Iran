๐นSeventh Principle (of the Iranian Constitution)

The President is elected by an absolute majority of votes. In the event that no candidate receives an absolute majority of the votes in the first round, a second round is held on the following Friday, between the two candidates who received the highest number of votes in the first round.

The voting begins at the invitation of the government. The new President must be elected at least forty days and no more than sixty days before the end of the term of the incumbent President.

In the event of the absence of the President for any reason, or with the approval of his/her excuse by the Constitutional Council and the approval of an absolute majority of its members, who shall convene upon the invitation of the government, the temporary duties of the President shall be assumed by the Speaker of the Senate, except in cases provided for in Principles 11 and 12 of this law. If the Speaker of the Senate is also excused from carrying out these duties, the government shall assume responsibility for them.
In the event of the absence of the President or when the Constitutional Council definitively declares the President's excuse, the election of a new President shall be held at least twenty days and no more than thirty-five days after the vacancy, unless the Constitutional Council declares a state of emergency.

If, during the seven days preceding the deadline for the introduction of Presidential candidates, a person who has announced his/her candidacy no later than sixty days before that date dies or becomes incapacitated, the Constitutional Council shall decide on the postponement of the elections.

If one of the candidates dies or becomes incapacitated before the first round, the Constitutional Council shall announce the postponement of the elections.

If one of the two candidates who received the highest votes in the first round dies or becomes incapacitated, the Constitutional Council shall announce the postponement of the second round, and new candidates shall be introduced in accordance with the regulations.
